Our Mission

Sabra Shah Quran Academy is dedicated to providing authentic Islamic education and fostering a deep connection with the Quran. We strive to create an environment where students can learn, grow, and develop a strong foundation in their faith.

Our mission is to nurture a deep love for the Qur'an in the hearts of our community in Glasgow — and beyond — by providing high-quality tajweed, tafseer, and Islamic education for children, adults, and new Muslims
