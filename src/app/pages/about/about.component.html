A Legacy of Qur'an Teaching

The Qur'an Academy is built upon the lifelong dedication of Sabra Shah, a pillar of the Glasgow community, who devoted over 60 years to teaching the Qur'an, touching countless lives and inspiring generations.

For decades, she taught with sincerity, patience, and compassion. She spent 45 years teaching in Glasgow, including 35 years at the UKIM Glasgow Branch. She welcomed children, adults, and new Muslims into her classes. Thousands of students benefited from her teaching, many of whom still recite and pass on the Qur'an today.

To continue her mission, the Qur'an Academy has been established at 70 Coplaw Street, Glasgow.

This centre will be a safe and welcoming space for:

  • Children beginning their Qur'an journey
  • Adults reconnecting with their faith
  • New Muslims seeking support in tajweed, tafseer, and Islamic studies

It is more than a building — it is a place where hearts will be connected to the Qur'an, and where her legacy will live on as sadaqah jariyah, in shā' Allāh.
